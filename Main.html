Information About MSRTC

The Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation was established by the State Government of Maharashtra as per the provision in Section 3 of RTC Act 1950. The MSRTC operates its services by the approved scheme of road transport published vide Notification MVA 3173/30303-XIIA dated 29 November 1973 in the official gazette.

The Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation abbreviated as (MSRTC, or simply ST),[1] is the state run bus service of Maharashtra, India which serves routes to towns and cities within Maharashtra as well as to its adjoining states

Recently From 21st May 2020, the Corporation Started Goods Transportation, Private Bus Body Building, Private Vehicle Tyre Remoulding.[3] In Future Corporation will start Petrol Pump for Private Vehicles all over the Maharashtra

MSRTC is operating a fleet of approximately 18,449 buses that ferry 8.7 million passengers daily.. It also offers a facility for online booking of tickets for all buses. The MSRTC is one of the biggest public transport bodies in the country with a fleet of around 93,000 employees.

The Board of Directors of Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation has a Chairman and a maximum of 17 Directors. The Board consists of Vice President and Managing Director, 9 Associate Directors, 3 Central and 2 State Government Representatives and 2 Labor Representatives. Directors have been appointed

MSRTC offers a large number of buses to passengers including semi-luxury Hirkani, Night Express, AC-Shivneri, AC-Shivshahi and semi-sleeper buses. Buses are Parivartan, Day Ordinary are also quite popular among travelers.
